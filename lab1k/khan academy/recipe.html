ü•û Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Details

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Cook on a hot pan until bubbles appear.
  4. Flip and cook until golden.
